Women of the Word

Women of the Word seeks to empower women to make a difference in their congregations and in the world at large.

Mary Circle

The Mary Circle is a group of women, who meet regularly for Bible Study and Fellowship. Meetings are on Wednesday mornings from 9:30am to 11:00am in the Church Library.

Saturday Brunch at La Madeline

The Ladies of First Presbyterian Church and their friends meet for Brunch every Third Saturday of the month at La Madeline in Allen. See you at 10:00 on the Third Saturday!

Men's Ministry

At FPCA, we encourage men to live a Christian life, especially at work and home. This ministry supports men in their mission to become fully-mature, devoted followers of Jesus Christ and godly servant-leaders in their home and community

Men's Fellowship Breakfast

Men of FPCA meet the first and third Tuesday of each month at Allen Cafe, 302 E. Main Street, at 6:30 am for fellowship, conversation and networking.

Study

First Presbyterian Church offers a variety of opportunities to all ages for study and reflection. Our schedule changes throughout the calendar year. Current Classes Include:

  • Children - UpLift for Kids during Sunday morning worship.

  • Youth - Weekly lessons with Pastor Kelly – location announced each week.

  • Adults - Wednesday evenings at 6:00pm – Lectio Divina Class – this gathering centers around four (4) different Scripture readings each week. Participants slowly read each lesson, looking for words or phrases that speak to them. Discussion includes a time for questions, comments and sharing what we have learned from the passages. This class meets in the Church Library.
